URL Shortener Microservice

EXAMPLE

Creation:

https://url-shortener-microservice-tylenis.c9users.io/new/http://www.freecodecamp.com
https://url-shortener-microservice-tylenis.c9users.io/new/https://www.google.com

Output

{"original_url":"http://www.freecodecamp.com","short_url":"https://url-shortener-microservice-tylenis.c9users.io/59"}
{"original_url":"https://www.google.com","short_url":"https://url-shortener-microservice-tylenis.c9users.io/60"}

https://url-shortener-microservice-tylenis.c9users.io/59
https://url-shortener-microservice-tylenis.c9users.io/60
